Why
Attend

Whether you are new to Flutter or an experienced user, Flutter BootCamp will give you inspiration & technical knowledge for your software development journey. We have put together a mix of thought leaders & Flutter experts to cover topics like On-boarding, Firebase Integration, App States, APIs and play store deployment best practices.

The purpose of the bootcamp is to network, share ideas & have meaningful conversations about how others are implementing Flutter in their organizations and more
